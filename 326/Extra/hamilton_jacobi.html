Basic motivation

We begin with the notion of a phase space. At time ttt we speak of a dynamical system as being in some state that lives in this phase space. Classically, this state is specified by providing a generalized coordinate qiq_iqi​ along with an associated (conjuage) momentum pip_ipi​. The canonical equations are given by
